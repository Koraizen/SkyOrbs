1Ô∏è‚É£ Inside Ore Generation

Ores only generate inside the solid volume of the planet (never outside or in air).

For solid planets: ores fill the mantle/core zones.

For hollow planets: ores appear only in the shell layer between the hollow space and the surface.

Configurable per biome and ore type:

ores:
enabled: true
layers:
- name: "common"
ores:
- type: "IRON_ORE"
chance: 0.3
vein_size: [2, 6]
depth_range: [0.2, 0.8] # from surface inward
- name: "rare"
ores:
- type: "DIAMOND_ORE"
chance: 0.05
vein_size: [1, 4]
depth_range: [0.5, 0.7]

Replaces only replaceable rock materials, never air or structure blocks.

Supports custom core inclusions:

core:
type: "MAGMA_BLOCK"
rare_ore_inclusions: [ANCIENT_DEBRIS]
inclusion_chance: 0.01

8Ô∏è‚É£ Grounded Flora

Plants, trees, and decorations never float.

When placing vegetation:

Check downward until a valid ground block is found (stone, grass, etc.).

Skip if no ground within range.

9Ô∏è‚É£ Satellites, Rings, and Asteroids

Each planet can have satellites, moons, or glowing rings.

Rings made of small rock clusters or particles for performance.

Configurable count, distance, and brightness:

max_count: 3
materials: [STONE, DEEPSLATE, GLOWSTONE]
ring_chance: 0.2
